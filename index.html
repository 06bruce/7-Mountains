CLUBS

Our Pillas

BUSINESS CLUB

The business club in the software department focuses on merging technical expertise with entrepreneurial skills. It helps students explore real-world applications of software development, fostering innovation, leadership, and business strategy insights within the tech industry.

ENGLISH CLUB

The English club in the IT department enhances communication skills for tech students, focusing on professional writing, presentations, and interviews. It empowers members to articulate complex technical concepts clearly, boosting their confidence in global tech environments.

IT CLUB

IT clubs offer hands-on experience with coding, cybersecurity, and AI, helping students sharpen their technical skills. They foster collaboration, innovation, and practical problem-solving in the tech field.

Media CLUB

Music clubs provide a creative outlet for students to explore various genres, collaborate on performances, and develop their musical talents. They foster a sense of community and artistic expression within the campus environment.

CHURCHES AND FAMILIES

Churches and families serve as foundational pillars, offering guidance, moral values, and a sense of belonging. They nurture individuals through spiritual growth, mutual support, and community engagement.

NGOs CLUB

NGO clubs work on social impact projects, addressing issues like education and environmental conservation. They empower members to drive meaningful change and contribute to community well-being.

EDUCATION CLUB

Education clubs in schools provide students with opportunities to explore their passions, apply knowledge in practical settings, and develop skills like collaboration and leadership outside the classroom..
